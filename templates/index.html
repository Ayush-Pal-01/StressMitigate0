Box Breathing

Calm your nervous system with this simple 4-4-4-4 breathing pattern.

Get Ready...
🧘

Body Scan Meditation

Slowly bring awareness to each part of your body, releasing tension as you go.

1. Close your eyes and take 3 deep breaths.
2. Notice your feet — any tension? Let it go.
3. Move to your legs, hips, stomach — breathe into each.
4. Feel your chest, shoulders, arms — release.
5. Relax your face, jaw, forehead. You're at peace.
🌍

5-4-3-2-1 Grounding

Use your senses to anchor yourself to the present moment.

5 things you can see
4 things you can touch
3 things you can hear
2 things you can smell
1 thing you can taste
💪

Progressive Muscle Relaxation

Tense and release muscle groups to physically release stress.

1. Clench your fists tightly for 5 seconds, then release.
2. Scrunch your shoulders to your ears, hold, release.
3. Tighten your stomach muscles, hold, release.
4. Squeeze your legs together, hold, release.
5. Curl your toes tightly, hold, release. Notice the calm.
📝

Mindful Journaling

Write freely for 5 minutes. No judgment, just let your thoughts flow.

Pick a prompt: "Right now I feel..." or "I'm grateful for..."
Set a 5-minute timer and write without stopping.
Don't edit. Don't judge. Just flow.
When done, take a deep breath and close the page.

Frequently Asked Questions

What does StressMitigate do?

StressMitigate is an AI-powered mental health companion that detects emotional stress through your text, voice, and facial expressions to provide personalized support.

Is my data private?

Yes. Your data is stored securely and encrypted. No human reads your sessions. You control which modalities are active.

How is my Wellness Score calculated?

Your score is based on your check-in moods (40%), AI session stress levels (40%), and daily engagement (20%). It's a 7-day rolling average.

Can the AI replace a therapist?

No. StressMitigate is a supportive companion, not a replacement for professional mental health care. If you're in crisis, please reach out to a professional.
